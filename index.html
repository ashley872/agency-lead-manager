How to Use Agency Clay

What is this?
Your team's lead management & brand discovery CRM. Import leads, auto-score them, discover new brands with AI, pull contacts, push to outreach campaigns, and track everything in one place.

The 4 Tabs

Leads Tab — Your contact database
Every person you're targeting lives here. Import from CSV (Apollo, LinkedIn, any spreadsheet), or add manually.
  • Import CSV — auto-detects Apollo, LinkedIn, and generic formats
  • Auto-Clean — AI normalizes names, titles, countries, detects email types, removes junk
  • Verify Emails — checks bounceability via MillionVerifier
  • Scoring — each lead scored 0-100 based on title, industry, company size, signals
  • Tiers — A (Hot), B (Warm), C (Nurture), D (Enrich), F (Dead)
  • Pipeline view — Kanban board to visualize stages
  • Duplicates — imports skip existing emails automatically, never duplicates
Brands Tab — Company-level view
Brands are auto-created from your leads (grouped by company name). Shows TikTok Shop data, FMCG status, pitch angles.
  • TTS Status Check — looks up each brand on TikTok Shop via FastMoss
  • Find Contacts — search Apollo for decision-makers at any brand
  • FMCG Detection — AI checks if brands are FMCG/CPG or not
  • CPG Conglomerates — auto-detects if a brand belongs to Unilever, L'Oréal, P&G, etc.
  • K-Beauty tags — flags Korean beauty brands automatically
  • Export — CSV or LinkedIn Ads Account Match format
Discovery Tab — AI-powered brand finding
Find new target brands automatically, let AI decide if they're worth pursuing, then pull contacts.
  • Search Apollo — finds beauty/skincare/wellness/DTC companies by size & industry
  • Search TTS — finds active TikTok Shop sellers via FastMoss
  • Watchlist — paste brand names/URLs manually for AI to evaluate
  • AI Qualify All — Claude scores each brand 0-100 on: TTS readiness, category fit, revenue fit, competition gap. Brands scoring 60+ are auto-approved
  • Auto-Pull Contacts — finds decision-makers (CEO, CMO, VP Marketing, etc.) at approved brands via Apollo, scores them, and adds to your Leads tab
  • LinkedIn Ads CSV — export approved brands for LinkedIn matched audience ads
Reporting Tab — Analytics & monitoring
  • Dashboard — pipeline funnel, top brand opportunities, tier breakdown
  • Activity Log — tracks all major actions (imports, exports, AI runs, syncs, team access)
  • SmartLead Stats — campaign performance (sent, opened, replied, clicked)
  • Brand Monitoring — track TTS performance over time, detect GMV changes

Quick Start for New Users

1. Import your leads
Leads tab → Import CSV. Upload your Apollo/LinkedIn exports. The tool auto-maps columns. Hit confirm, then run Auto-Clean to score & route everything.
2. Check your pipeline
Use sidebar filters (stage, tier, industry, country) to find leads. Switch to Pipeline view for Kanban. Click any lead to edit, change stage, add notes.
3. Export & outreach
Export filtered leads as CSV, Beehiiv newsletter format, or LinkedIn Ads matched audience format. Or push directly to SmartLead for email campaigns.
4. Dream 100 & Campaign Routing
Enterprise/global brands auto-route to Dream 100 — they're excluded from SmartLead auto-push so you can send custom, structured outreach. In Settings, map different SmartLead campaigns per tier (A/B/C) and newsletter segment. When you push, leads route to the right campaign automatically.
5. Enterprise Brand Workflow ("Brand In → Contacts Out")
  1. Go to Discovery → Watchlist and type the brand name (e.g. "Estee Lauder")
  2. Run AI Qualify All to research and score the brand
  3. Approve the brand, then click Find Contacts
  4. Use Enterprise Role Presets in the dropdown: Social Commerce, Digital/DTC Leaders, Growth & Performance, Influencer & Creator, Marketing Leadership
  5. Apollo returns the right people at that brand, auto-scored and added to Leads
  6. Edit each lead → fill in Personalization Hook (one sentence of real research) and Research Brief (for Dream 100)
  7. Outreach manually for Dream 100, or push to SmartLead with {{Personalization_Hook}} and {{Proof_Point}} merge tags
6. Newsletter Segments
Newsletter leads are auto-segmented: New Sellers (no TTS), Active Sellers (on TTS), Agency (consultant titles), Enterprise (200+ employees / $50M+ rev). Use sidebar sub-filters to view each segment. Export by segment for targeted Beehiiv campaigns.

Team Sync

Data syncs across the team via Supabase. Your API keys (Apollo, Claude, FastMoss, etc.) are shared automatically — you don't need to enter them individually.

Push to Cloud — sends your local data to the shared database
Pull from Cloud — downloads the latest shared data to your browser

The sync indicator (top right) shows connection status. Auto-sync happens in the background when you make changes.

Good to Know

  • Imports never duplicate — if an email already exists, it's skipped
  • Auto-Clean is safe to re-run — it only changes uncleaned leads
  • All data is in your browser + Supabase — export anytime as backup
  • Activity Log (Reporting tab) tracks all major actions
