Why Now for Hoover?

  • Hoover already has a TikTok presence — @hooveruk with 37K followers — demonstrating organic brand interest and content potential
  • The Home & Living category is growing rapidly but remains under-penetrated vs. Beauty and Fashion, meaning early movers capture disproportionate share
  • Vacuum cleaners are highly visual, demonstrable products — ideal for short-form video and live shopping formats
  • Competitors like SharkNinja are already on TikTok Shop and seeing explosive results (Shark Steam Mop: 5,800% order increase in Spring 2025)
  • Hoover's £1M UK monthly turnover and hero products (cordless, robot, handheld) are perfectly suited for TikTok's impulse-buy demographic
  • TikTok Shop launched across wider Europe on 31 March 2025 — the window for first-mover advantage is closing

The Gap

SharkNinja is the clear category leader on TikTok Shop, running full-funnel social commerce including Shop Ads, creator affiliates, and regular live shopping events. Dyson has strong organic buzz but no official Shop presence — choosing to stay premium/off-platform. This creates a direct opening for Hoover to capture the mid-market between Shark's aggressive TikTok presence and Dyson's absence.

  • Hoover's price point (lower than Dyson, competitive with Shark) is ideal for TikTok's impulse-buy behaviour
  • Cordless and robot vacuums are among the most "demo-friendly" products for short video
  • No established UK vacuum brand besides Shark is running a serious creator affiliate program on TikTok
  • Hoover's brand heritage (trusted, household name) gives it credibility that newer DTC brands lack

Proposed Launch Roadmap

A phased approach to launching and scaling Hoover's TikTok Shop presence, designed to deliver early wins while building sustainable, long-term growth.

Phase 1 — Days 1–30

Foundation & Shop Setup

Get the shop live, catalogue optimised, and first content in production.

  • TikTok Shop account setup and verification
  • Product catalogue build — hero products first (cordless, robot, handheld)
  • Shopify integration and order flow configuration
  • Initial batch of 10–15 shoppable videos produced
  • Creator outreach begins — target 20–30 home/cleaning creators
  • Shop Ads account setup and pixel configuration
Phase 2 — Days 30–60

Creator Network & Content Scale

Activate the affiliate programme and scale content production.

  • Affiliate programme live — 15+ creators producing content
  • First live shopping events (2x per week)
  • Paid amplification on top-performing organic content (Spark Ads)
  • Product Shopping Ads running on hero SKUs
  • Content calendar established — 3–5 videos per week from creators + in-house
  • Performance baseline established for optimisation
Phase 3 — Days 60–90

Scale & Optimise

Full-funnel operation running — optimise based on data and scale what works.

  • Creator network scaled to 30–50 active affiliates
  • Live shopping events increased to 3–4x per week
  • Full product catalogue live (including mattress cleaners, accessories)
  • ROAS-optimised ad campaigns across all formats
  • Seasonal campaign planning (summer cleaning, back to school, Black Friday prep)
  • Monthly performance review and strategy refinement
